About MandarinMe

MandarinMe is a web application designed to support self-taught Mandarin learners through the HSK1-HSK3 levels. The platform offers structured study tools, vocabulary practice, progress tracking, sentence-building exercises, and personalized learning features to help users stay consistent and motivated on their language-learning journey.
